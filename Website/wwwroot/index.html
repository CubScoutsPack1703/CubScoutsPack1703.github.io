Cub Scouts is filled with unique and diverse experiences. We welcome girls and boys in kindergarten to fifth grade to join us.

Each month, we have 1 Pack meeting (for all grades, usually on the second Tuesday of the month), 2 Den meetings (grade specific, schedule is set by Den Leader), 1 event (usually a campout, outing, or activity), and an optional hike (or bike ride).

About Our Pack

Cub Scouts Pack 1703 is a family Pack, which means we welcome girls and boys in kindergarten to fifth grade from Troy, Auburn Hills, Rochester Hills, Birmingham, and the surrounding communities.

Most of our Den and Pack meetings are held at Leonard Elementary School. Ceremonies and special events are often held at Stage Nature Center, our charter organization.
