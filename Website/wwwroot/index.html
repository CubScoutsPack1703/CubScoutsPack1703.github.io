About Our Pack

Cub Scouts Pack 1703 is a family Pack, which means we welcome girls and boys in kindergarten to fifth grade from Troy, Auburn Hills, Rochester Hills, Birmingham, and the surrounding communities.

We have a large Pack with Cub Scouts from many local schools including:

  • Leonard Elementary School
  • Hamilton Elementary School
  • Martell Elementary School
  • Schroeder Elementary School
  • Bemis Elementary School
  • Woodland Elementary School
  • GATE Magnet School
  • Roeper Elementary School

Most of our Den and Pack meetings are held at Leonard Elementary School, since it is a central location for our Cub Scouts. Ceremonies and special events are often held at Stage Nature Center, our charter organization.
